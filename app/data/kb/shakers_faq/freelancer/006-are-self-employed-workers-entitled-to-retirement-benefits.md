# Are self-employed workers entitled to retirement benefits?

**Q:** Are self-employed workers entitled to retirement benefits?

**A:** Yes, self-employed workers are entitled to a pension as they are required to contribute to Social Security, and these contributions entitle them to receive a retirement pension in the future.
